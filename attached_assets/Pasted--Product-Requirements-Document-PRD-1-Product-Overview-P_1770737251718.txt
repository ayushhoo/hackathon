# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** van sampatti
**Product Type:** Data-driven environmental monitoring and restoration platform with a public-facing website

**Vision:**
To enable real-time, data-informed restoration and management of riparian zones using scalable sensing infrastructure, AI-driven analytics, and community engagement.

**Mission:**
Empower environmental stakeholders with precise, real-time insights and predictive tools to combat riparian degradation, deforestation, runoff, and habitat disruption.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Riparian zones are critical ecological buffers but are increasingly threatened by deforestation, erosion, runoff, climate variability, and delayed intervention. Current monitoring methods are:

* Manual and labor-intensive
* Infrequent and reactive
* Lacking predictive and simulation capabilities

There is a need for a **scalable, automated, and intelligent system** that continuously monitors riparian health, detects anomalies early, and supports adaptive restoration strategies.

---

## 3. Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goals

* Enable real-time monitoring of riparian ecosystems
* Provide AI-driven insights and actionable restoration recommendations

### Success Metrics

## 5. Core Features

### 5.1 Automated Sensing & Monitoring

**Description:**
A network of stationary, IoT-connected LiDAR sensors integrated with environmental sensors.

**Key Capabilities:**

* LiDAR-based terrain and vegetation mapping
* Soil moisture monitoring
* Water quality sensing (turbidity, pH, contaminants)
* Continuous real-time data ingestion

---

### 5.2 AI-Driven Analytics

**Description:**
Autonomous machine learning models analyze multimodal sensor data to deliver insights and predictions.

**Capabilities:**

* Anomaly detection (erosion, vegetation loss, flooding risk)
* Crop and native vegetation suitability prediction
* Rainfall and runoff forecasting
* Adaptive management recommendations

---

### 5.3 Digital Twin & Predictive Simulation

**Description:**
A virtual replica of monitored riparian zones for simulation and planning.

**Capabilities:**

* Scenario testing (replanting strategies, climate events)
* Long-term restoration outcome forecasting
* Iterative refinement using real-time feedback loops
